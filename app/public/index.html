Shesh Besh Smoke Dungeon

Submitted by Lubomir on 4/5/17

Nazareth's best loved smoky sheshbesh dungeon, nestled in the heart of the old town, is a popular hangout among the city's unemployed males. Be struck by the classic interior architecture, while enjoying a warm cup of cinammon tea with crushed pecans, if you so fancy.

Find it on the right when coming from the small open square containing discarded furniture.
